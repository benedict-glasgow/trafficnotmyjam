FAQ

  • Q : Why can’t I see who has commented?
  • A: Comments on Traffic-Not-My-Jam are anonymized! Other users can’t see what you have commented to discourage arguments.

  • Q: Can people see if I have made a post?
  • A: Yes, People will be able to see the username of who has made the post.

  • Q: I can’t make a comment? How do I do this?
  • A: You can’t make a comment unless you are registered and signed into an account. The comment box will not be visible to user who are not logged in.

  • Q: Can I react to a post without an account?
  • A: Yes, everyone can react to posts. No account is needed.
